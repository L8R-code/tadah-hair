What It Is

Beaded Weft
Extensions

Beaded weft extensions use machine-sewn wefts of 100% human hair attached to your natural hair using micro-beads — no heat tools, no adhesive whatsoever. The machine weft is thicker and more structured than a hand-tied weft, which means it delivers serious volume quickly. The beads are clamped into place leaving zero chemical residue and cause zero damage when removed correctly. It's one of the most versatile methods Jewels offers — clients with fine hair, thick hair, wavy hair, and straight hair have all gotten outstanding results with this method.

What to Expect

The Process

  1. 01

    Free Consult

    Jewels assesses your hair health, density, and goals. She confirms beaded weft is your best method, does your color match, and builds your full custom plan.

  2. 03

    Bead Placement

    Small micro-beads are placed in precise rows. Exact positioning depends on where you need volume and length. No chemicals, no heat involved.

  3. 04

    Weft Install

    Machine wefts are threaded through the beads and clamped. The result is flat, full, and moves naturally. Most installs take 2–4 hours.

  4. 05

    Move-Up

    Every 8–12 weeks, beads are repositioned as your natural hair grows. Your extension hair is fully reused as long as it's been properly cared for.
