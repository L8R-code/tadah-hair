What It Is

Hand-Tied Weft
Extensions

Hand-tied weft extensions use wefts of 100% human hair that are sewn together by hand — not machine — then attached to your natural hair using small micro-beads. No heat, no glue, no adhesive of any kind. Because the wefts are hand-sewn, they lie completely flat against the head, making them virtually undetectable even when hair is worn up. They're the most in-demand professional method for clients with fine, thin, or color-treated hair who need a gentle option that still delivers serious transformation. Jewels is also IBE (Invisible Bead Extensions) certified — a specific hand-tied method known for its especially flat, flexible result.

Ideal For
  • ✦Fine or thin hair that can't handle adhesive or heat bonds
  • ✦Color-treated or chemically processed hair needing a gentle approach
  • ✦Clients who frequently wear their hair up — wefts are flat and invisible in updos
  • ✦Anyone who wants maximum volume with the least possible weight

What to Expect

The Process

  1. 01

    Free Consult

    Hair health assessment, density check, and confirmation that hand-tied weft is your best method. Color matching and full plan built at this appointment.

  2. 03

    Bead Placement

    Small micro-beads are placed in rows along your natural hair. Exact placement depends on where you need volume and length. No chemicals, no heat.

  3. 04

    Weft Attachment

    Hand-tied wefts are threaded through the beads and secured. Completely flat, flexible, and moves naturally with your hair from day one.

  4. 05

    Move-Up

    Every 8–12 weeks, beads are moved up to account for growth. Your extension hair is fully reused — you're paying for the service, not new hair every time.
