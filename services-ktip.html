What It Is

K-Tip Keratin Bond
Extensions

K-Tip extensions — also called keratin bond or fusion extensions — are individual strands of 100% human hair attached to your natural hair using a keratin protein bond applied with a precision heat tool. Each strand is placed one at a time, so Jewels can put volume and length exactly where you need it. The result moves, feels, and behaves exactly like your natural hair. Nobody will know unless you tell them.

Ideal For
  • ✦Clients who want the most natural-looking, undetectable result

What to Expect

The Process

  1. 01

    Free Consult

    Jewels assesses your hair health, density, and goals. She color-matches and confirms K-Tip is right for your hair type.

  2. 03

    Custom Order

    Your extensions are ordered to spec — correct length, weight, and color blend. No pre-packaged inventory.

  3. 04

    Install Day

    Allow 3–5 hours. Each strand is individually bonded. You leave with full movement, a seamless blend, and a complete aftercare walkthrough.

  4. 05

    Move-Up

    Every 3–5 months, bonds are removed and reinstalled as your natural hair grows. Hair can often be reused if well maintained.

Common Questions

K-Tip Keratin Bond
FAQs

When applied and removed correctly by a certified stylist, K-Tip extensions do not damage healthy hair. Damage occurs from improper installation, skipping maintenance, or attempting removal at home. Jewels only installs on hair she has assessed as healthy — if yours isn't ready, she'll tell you and give you a plan to get there.
Most K-Tip installs take 3–5 hours depending on how much hair is being placed. Jewels never rushes — your appointment is blocked with the full time needed.
Extensions can be professionally colored, but Jewels typically recommends selecting from the color range at your consultation to minimize chemical processing. She'll advise what's safe for your specific hair at your consult.
K-Tips are individual strands bonded one at a time — maximum placement precision and the most natural movement. Tape-ins are wefts attached with adhesive. K-Tips generally last 3–5 months between move-ups vs. 6–8 weeks for tape-in, and offer more customization.
No. K-Tip bonds must be professionally removed using a bond remover solution that protects your natural hair. Attempting home removal will cause damage.
